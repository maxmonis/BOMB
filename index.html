Gameplay

The first player starts the game by naming a movie. The next player then names an actor from the movie. The following player then names another movie starring that actor. Play continues this way until a player is stumped, at which point they can challenge. The previous player then has to name another actor from the previous movie or another movie starring the previous actor. If they succeed, the challenging player receives a letter, and if not, the challenged player receives a letter. A new round then begins. Players are eliminated once they've spelled out the word BOMB, and the game ends when all players but one have been eliminated. That player is the winner!

Actors and movies cannot be reused throughout the entire game. If a player provides an incorrect response, the next player has the option of marking that response as incorrect, at which time the player who named the incorrect movie or actor will immediately receive a letter and a new round will begin.

Creating and Joining Games

You can create a game by entering your name. As the game creator, you will then be able to approve or reject join requests from other players, and also determine when to start your game. Once a game has started, no one else can join. To request to join an existing game, simply click its "Request to Join" button and enter your name. You can optionally also add a message to the creator. The creator will then admit or reject you. You can play one game at a time.
